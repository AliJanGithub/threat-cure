## **Understanding Common Security Pain Points**

Today’s businesses operate in complex and dynamic environments. The rapid shift from on-premises infrastructure to cloud and hybrid models has significantly expanded the attack surface. As a result, organizations encounter several recurring security challenges.

---

## **Data Breaches and Data Loss**

Data is the lifeblood of modern organizations. A single breach can result in severe financial losses, reputational harm, and legal consequences. Common causes of data breaches include:

- **Human Error:**  
  Accidental exposure or mishandling of sensitive information.

- **Phishing Attacks:**  
  Tricking employees into revealing credentials or confidential data.

- **Malware Infections:**  
  Malicious software compromising systems and data.

- **Weak Passwords:**  
  Easily guessable or reused credentials.

- **Unpatched Vulnerabilities:**  
  Exploitable flaws in outdated software.

ThreatCure addresses these risks through advanced monitoring, threat intelligence, and proactive security controls.

- **Proactive Detection Systems:**

- **Incident Response and Recovery:**

## **Insider Threats**

Insider threats remain one of the most challenging risks for organizations. Employees, contractors, or privileged users may unintentionally or maliciously misuse their access, leading to data leaks, intellectual property theft, or system disruption.

ThreatCure mitigates insider threats with:

- **ShieldOps Advanced Data Hunting Platform:**  
  Detects anomalous behavior indicative of potential insider activity.

- **Breach and Attack Simulation:**  
  Tabletop exercises and drill simulations to prepare technical teams for real incidents.

- **Zero-Trust Framework:**  
  Enforces least-privilege access by limiting users to only what is necessary.

- **Security Awareness Programs:**  
  Educates employees on security best practices and risk awareness.
